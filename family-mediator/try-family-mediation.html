What Is Family Mediation, and Why Are So Many Turning to It?

In times of personal crisis a separation, divorce or battle over children or property families don’t just sign up to legal advice. They require understanding, neutrality and a forward path that avoids escalating conflict. That’s where Local Family Mediators comes in.

An expert and impartial mediator assists both sides in finding common ground and making decisions, that are fair to everyone involved, during this process. It’s not a contest, but rather It’s about determining how to move forward with grace and consideration.

Why Is Mediation Better Than Going To Court With Your Family Disputes?

One of the questions families frequently ask is “Is family mediation better than court?” and for good reason. The answer is yes, at least for most cases.

Here’s why:

Faster resolution: In court, cases can drag on for months at a time, while mediation sessions might be able to begin within days.

Savings: The cost of a lawyer and court can become expensive rapidly. (See On-line Divorce Mediation) Local Family Mediators is also less expensive.

Broad powers: Whether your family will have a future is up to a court judge. You do in mediation.

Who can beneﬁt from family mediation?

So many of our clients want to know, “What does family mediation actually looks like?” It’s easier and more intimate than you think.

Barker Mediation Coventry has a clear structure to the sessions as follows:

  • First meeting: Each party has a private session with the mediator.

  • Joint sessions: The mediator brings all parties together to talk.

  • Agreement drafting: In the event of a resolution, we draft a written explanation.

Everything discussed remains confidential. Mediation is not therapy but many of those who use it describe it as a healing process.

Is Family Mediation Legally Binding?

One common question we hear is, “Is family mediation legally binding?” And to that the answer is, yes it can be. The answer: it can.

The agreements you reach in family mediation are not automatically legal binding, but they can be turned into a Consent Order with the court. At Barker Mediation Coventry, we have relationships with experienced lawyers who can help you finalise your agreement in necessary.

And since you tailored the contract yourselves, your chances of adhering to it are greater sparing yourself future discord.
