What Do Coventry Local Family Barkers Mediation Offer?

It’s been our privilege to help many, many families in hard times. Families encounter all kinds of challenges from contentious separations and parenting disputes to property issues. We’re here to help, no matter what you’re going through. Here are eight ways we’re here to help families when things get hard:

Child Arrangements Mediation

Helping parents negotiate on where the children will live and how their time with both parents will be divided, where the kids go to school, holiday arrangements but being child-focused.

Financial Mediation

Assisting couples to achieve a fair or transparent resolution on savings, debt, dividing the property, pensions and spousal support following their separation and divorce.

Divorce and Separation Mediation

Guiding partners in transition through emotionally and legally challenging transitions, maintaining a calm, respectful and future oriented process.

MIAMs(Mediation Information & Assessment Meetings)

The beginning of the mediation process. Preceded by this mandatory one-on-one session with a mediator, the voluntary process determines whether mediation is the right match.

Co-Parenting Communication Support

Providing parents with communication skills and guidance to use the most effective means of communicating after a separation for their children’s sake as well as their own.

Property and Inheritance Disputes

Mediating for those with extended family members, siblings, or exes whom they wish to resolve property disputes and/or inheritance miscommunications.

Grandparent Access Mediation

Assisting grandparents and other relatives to minimise conflict and establish contact or residence orders in relation to grandchildren.

Online Family Mediation

Providing secure video mediation sessions to accommodate families who are pressed for time and who live far apart.

There are always real problems families need to work through and we provide those working with family needs a compassionate professional and tailored experience, which is what sets our team of Locally Based Family Mediators apart from the rest.
